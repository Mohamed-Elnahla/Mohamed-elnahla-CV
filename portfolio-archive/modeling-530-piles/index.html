Modeling 530 Piles Using Dynamo Scripts

  • Created By: Mohamed Elnahla
  • Date: January 2021
  • Categories: Structural BIM Modeling

Modeling 530 Piles with different cut levels on Revit and giving them IDs as a Shared Parameter and Tagging them on a Plan View in Less than 2 Minutes using Dynamo and coordinates from Excel Spreadsheet, Also Creating Levels and Structural Plans Associated with it using Another Dynamo Based on Data from an Excel Spreadsheet

These Scripts are Created as a part of our Graduation Project of Construction Project Management to help us Modeling the world Largest Wastewater Treatment Plant (Bahr El Baqar Wastewater Treatment Plant)

We seek to use new technologies in Construction Management During our Project and that is just a small part as we begin working
